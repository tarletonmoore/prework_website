About Me

My name is Tarleton. I have a strong interest in everything that deals with Nintendo and horror. I have a huge Nintendo collection. My collection includes every console made, from NES to the Switch. I also have every handheld console. I usually play old school Nintendo games because they are very nostalgic to me, considering the first video game I ever played was Ocarina Of Time on the N64. As far as more modern games, I usually play survival horror. My favorite horror series would have to be Outlast, since it is the only game that has actually made me jump.
